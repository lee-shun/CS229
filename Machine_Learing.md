### Definition

- Supervised  Learning

- Regression Problem

  `The mapping is continuous.`

  

- Classification Problem

  `Discrete Outputs，like "1 or 0, Yes or No, Cat, Dog or Fish?" problems.`

  

- Support Vector Machine
